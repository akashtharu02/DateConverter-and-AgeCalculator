About This Website

Welcome to our Date Conversion and Age Calculator website! This platform is designed to help users seamlessly convert dates between the Gregorian calendar (AD) and the Bikram Sambat calendar (BS), which is widely used in Nepal. Additionally, you can calculate your exact age in years, months, and days by simply entering your date of birth.

Key Features

  • AD to BS Conversion: Quickly convert any date from the Gregorian calendar (AD) to the corresponding Bikram Sambat (BS) date
  • BS to AD Conversion: Easily convert dates from the Bikram Sambat (BS) calendar to the Gregorian (AD) format.
  • Age Calculator: Calculate your age accurately in years, months, and days by entering your date of birth.

Why This Website?

This website was developed to simplify the process of converting dates between these two calendar systems, which is especially useful for individuals who need to work with both calendars in daily life. Whether you're checking important dates, filling out forms, or simply curious, this tool offers accurate and reliable date conversion and age calculation features.

Our user-friendly design ensures that everyone can navigate the tools with ease, whether you're a student, professional, or just someone interested in knowing more about the Bikram Sambat calendar.
